Quirks of the Quack

This is a series of duck adventures that takes place in the territory of Ducklandia. With each new episode comes amazing new adventures. Tune in each week for some of the greatest stories of all time!

Actors protraying real ducks. No ducks were harmed in the filming of this series.

Pilot:

In a world where rubber ducks reign supreme, one unsuspecting bathtub toy finds itself in hot water, or rather, shark-infested waters! When rubber meets jaws, feathers fly and fins bite back. Will this duck sink or swim? Find out in this season's primere of "Quirks of the Quack."

Episode 2:

In this episode of "Quirks of the Quack" join our fabulous rubber ducks as they flock to the hottest party in town. But wait, who's that in the corner? Watch as the drama unfolds when one duck isn't as fabulous as the rest. Will it find a wingman, or is this lone duck destined for a night of quackloneliness? The pond is big, but the drama is even bigger!



Episode 3:

This time on "Quirks of the Quack" our rubbery friends find themselves in an unlikely clash with mischevious marshmellow chick peeps, lead by the ruthless General Peep. As tensions rise and marshmellow fluff flies, our rubber duck warriors must rally to defend Ducklandia. Alliances will be tested, friendships forged, but most importantly, the fate of both worlds hangs in the balance. Join us this episode where the battle will be anything but stale (the same can't be said about the peeps though).

Episode 4:

This time in Ducklandia, two ducks embark on the adventure of a lifetime! Join Daisy and Sir Quacks-a-lot as they unite together in the holy order of matrimony. As they waddle down the aisle, facing the judgmental stares of their plastic peers, one can only wonder if true love always floats to the top. Where duck'tails are served, and the dance floor is a splash zone, uncertainty lurks beneath the surface. Only time will tell if these two love-ducks will navigate the waves of married life or sccumb to the unpredictable waters.
